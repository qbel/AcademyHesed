DISTINCTIVES

We have some distinctives at Hesed Academy that set us apart from other private schools. These inclueds:

  1. Our curriculum is locally rooted with a global outlook
  2. In our curriculum, no field is left behind. We facilitate learning in science, Technology, Engineering, Arts and Mathematics.
  3. We operate a learner-centered philosophy of education with a hands-on approach to learning
  4. We consciously maintain small class size so students are recieve individualized attention
  5. Our prices are competitively favourable to the parents notwithstanding with what we offer. You recieve more than your money's worth.
  6. We operate on Christian values and ethics, with strong emphasis on Christian discipleship
  7. We are consciously raising future leaders for the market/public square.
  8. We orient our students towards transformational servant leadership and patriotism
  9. We have a competent, professional, loving, and caring faculty and staff team that models Christ-like character and professional competence for our students
