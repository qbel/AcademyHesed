Hesed Academy Vision

Competent Spirit-filled servant leaders transforming the diverse fields of human endeavour and society for human good and God’s glory.

Hesed Academy Mission

To provide a Christ-honoring community in a salutary environment that prepares young people for life-long commitment to learning, professional excellence, moral virtue, discipleship, and servant leadership in all areas of life.

Hesed Academy Mandate

To partner with parents in training and equipping their children as Christ’s ambassadors to transform His world into a free and prosperous society and to advance his kingdom through virtuous and just living, and through competency in their calling.
