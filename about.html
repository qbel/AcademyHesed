About

As a Christain private school, Hesed Academy is result driven, anchored in biblical truth, with sound academic pedigree, and is very affordable

As an academic institution, Hesed Academy, a K-12 school, offers exceptional educational services in a salutary learning environment. Hesed Academy operates a learner-centered philosophy of education, i.e., our students' overall best interest always remains the primary motivation for what we do. And, we adhere to the local education regulations and corporate business laws. Hesed Academy is wholly owned by Hesed Resource & Development Foundation (HESED RDF), a non-profit faith-based corporation that is focused on human capital development and societal transformation.

Pro Deo et Patria

Meaning: “For God and Country”

Hesed Academy Mandate

To partner with parents in training and equipping their children as Christ’s ambassadors to transform His world into a free and prosperous society and to advance his kingdom through virtuous and just living, and through competency in their calling.
